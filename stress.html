Avoiding Stress

I have decided to make a conscious effort to avoid getting stressed out. I say "conscious effort" because this doesn't come naturally to me, so I have to actively think about it.

There are a few reasons I want to avoid stress:

  1. I'm turning 28 on Tuesday. I spent most of my 20s feeling absolutely miserable.
  2. I have seen no indication that the things I stressed over were worth it.
  3. I've hurt other people by taking my stress out on them.
  4. The productivity that stress brings me is wiped away by the burnout that follows months later. A consistent pace would be more effective.

I have decided that it would be better to accomplish nothing if I can be happy and I can make others happy. I am very tired of my toxic ambition.

Lastly, I want to recognize that I am very fortunate to be in a privileged position where I even have the option of avoiding stress in my life.
